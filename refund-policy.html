Refund and Cancellation Policy

Your learning is 100% Risk-Free.

If you enroll in any of our programs but are either unable to attend or don't find it useful for your business, you will get a full refund of your investment — when you indicate within the stipulated time frame.

Section 1: General Refund Policy

1.1 Full Refund

We offer a full refund for certain programs if you are not satisfied or unable to attend, provided that you inform us within the specified time frames as detailed below.

1.2 Refund Process

To request a refund, you must contact our Customer Service team within the specified period. At your option, we can either apply your tuition to any other Zenogi program or send you a full refund to your bank account.

Section 2: Program-Specific Refund Policies

2.1 ZBS — Zenogi Business Systems

Refund Period: You must contact us before the final session of the program to claim a full refund.

Refund Eligibility: Refund requests made within this period will be initiated within 7–10 business days.

2.2 ZFC — Zenogi Founder Circle

Refund Period: You must contact us within 15 days from the beginning of the program.

Refund Eligibility: Refund requests made within this period will be processed for a full refund within 7 business days.

Section 3: No Refund Programs

The following program does not offer refunds or payment adjustments:

3.1 ZAP — Zenogi Apex Proximity

Due to the deeply personalised and invitation-only nature of this engagement, no refunds or payment adjustments are offered under any circumstances.

Section 4: Cancellation Policy

4.1 Cancellation Requests

Cancellation requests for ZBS and ZFC must be made at least 15 days before the start of the program.

4.2 Cancellation Process

To initiate a cancellation, please contact us on WhatsApp — include your cancellation request and the reason for it. Refund requests that meet the cancellation timeline will be processed and a full refund will be issued within 7–10 business days.

4.3 Non-Cancellable Programs

ZAP — Zenogi Apex Proximity does not permit cancellations or payment adjustments under any circumstances.

Section 5: Program Changes

5.1 Program Modification

Zenogi reserves the right to modify, reschedule, or cancel any program. If we cancel a program, you will be offered a full refund within 7 days of the cancellation announcement, or the option to transfer your tuition to another program.

Section 6: Contact

If you have any questions or need to request a refund, please contact us directly.

Section 7: Changes to This Policy

Zenogi reserves the right to modify this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the website and enrollment in programs after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
