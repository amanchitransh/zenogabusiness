Zenogi Business Programs

Refund and Commitment Policy

Commitment. Implementation. Long-Term Thinking.

Zenogi Business Programs are designed for serious businessmen, founders, and leaders who are committed to long-term strategic growth, implementation, leadership development, and higher-quality decision-making.

These programs are not casual educational products or passive-consumption courses. Participation requires active involvement, strategic focus, implementation effort, and professional commitment.

Participants are encouraged to carefully evaluate all program details before enrolling.

Section 1: Commitment Policy

By enrolling in any Zenogi program, you acknowledge and agree that you are making a serious professional commitment toward your business and personal growth.

All enrollment fees paid toward any Zenogi program are final and non-refundable.

This policy applies to all Zenogi Business Programs, including but not limited to:

  • ZBS — Zenogi Business Systems
  • ZFC — Zenogi Founder Circle
  • ZAP — Zenogi Apex Proximity

Section 2: Program Enrollment Policy

2.1 ZBS — Zenogi Business Systems

All enrollment fees paid for ZBS are final and non-refundable.

2.2 ZFC — Zenogi Founder Circle

Due to the limited and strategic nature of the Founder Circle, all enrollment fees are final and non-refundable.

2.3 ZAP — Zenogi Apex Proximity

ZAP is a highly selective, invitation-only strategic mentorship engagement with limited availability and reserved capacity.

All commitments and payments made toward ZAP are final and completely non-refundable.

Section 3: Missed Sessions and Participation

Failure to attend live sessions, participate actively, complete implementation work, or utilize the program resources does not qualify participants for refunds, credits, transfers, or payment adjustments.

Where applicable, session recordings or alternative access methods may be provided at the discretion of Zenogi.

Section 4: Program Access and Conduct

Zenogi reserves the right to deny, suspend, or terminate access to any participant whose behavior is disruptive, abusive, unethical, unlawful, or misaligned with the professional environment and values of the program.

In such cases, no refund, credit, or compensation will be provided.

Section 5: Program Modifications

Zenogi reserves the right to:

  • modify program structure,
  • update curriculum,
  • reschedule sessions,
  • change speakers or mentors,
  • improve content delivery,
  • or alter program format

at any time for the purpose of maintaining and improving program quality and effectiveness.

Such modifications shall not qualify for refunds, cancellations, or payment reversals.

Section 6: No Guaranteed Business Outcomes

Zenogi Business Programs provide strategic education, mentorship, leadership guidance, and implementation frameworks.

We do not guarantee:

  • specific revenue growth,
  • profitability,
  • business success,
  • client acquisition,
  • investment outcomes,
  • or any particular financial result.

Business results depend on multiple factors including implementation quality, market conditions, leadership capability, operational execution, and participant effort.

Section 7: Payment Disputes and Chargebacks

By enrolling in any Zenogi program, participants agree not to initiate unjustified payment disputes and chargebacks after receiving access to the program, onboarding materials, live sessions, recordings, mentorship, or related resources.

Any payment disputes shall first be addressed directly with the Zenogi support team.
